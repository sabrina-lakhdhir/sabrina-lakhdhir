Introduction to Information Visualization

This course provides students with an introduction to Information Visualization (or InfoVis for short). The students will learn principles of information representation, presentation and interaction, including development of mappings from data to visual structures and exploration, navigation, cues, distortion and emphasis techniques. While the students are asked to implement visualizations in programming code, the course focuses more on design of visualizations, than on implementation issues.
