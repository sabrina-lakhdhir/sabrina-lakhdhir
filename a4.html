Assignment 4: Create a Sculpture Composed of a Mix of Objects

Fall 2018

This assignment was completed in a group of three students. The goal was to create a sculpture of some sort which showcased multiple features including a scaffolding, a skin, a texutre, and a ______?
We attempted to create an image which showcases a bit of a story, or lifeline.
There are dead fish at the top, which would be caught by the whale, who is the main object in our image.
The whale also has seaweed in his mouth, along with fish feeding on those plants. The whale is then caught in a net, which represents being caught by humans, thus representing an entire cycle in nature.

Note: This assignment was completed in a group of three.
