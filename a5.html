Assignment 5: Leonardo da Vinci - Clock

Fall 2018

The goal of this assignment was to mimic Leonardo da Vinci's painting Vitruvian Man , but using ourselves and having the two sides of the clock contrast one another.
In my interpretation of this assignment, I decided to focus the attention on a single rose which is in full bloom on the upper half of the clock, but as you travel around the clock, it slowly starts to lose petals and die.
The contrasting colours also give a sense of darkness to the image and ensure emphasis is placed on the rose rather than on other objects in the image.
